About Us .

We are Gentle House for Interior Design and Decoration and it is one of the specialized institutions in the field of interior design and decoration engineering and its main headquarters is located in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia Jeddah Governorate in the Western Region and we are a multi-service institution where we have a history of accomplishments over 9 years of providing distinguished services we always care to discuss the opinions of our customers And to present ideas and suggestions through the designs that we offer, as we aim to build an integrated relationship with our customers by creating an atmosphere of cooperation, participation, innovation and supreme care in all parts of the details through our work period in the western region Of we have designed a lot of residential projects and administrative and commercial, as well as the hotel, and the most important thing distinguishes us is that we have great experience in materials and ways of working within the Saudi market, specifically the western region and the feature is compatibility design elements with what already exists in the market of materials and methods of installation.
